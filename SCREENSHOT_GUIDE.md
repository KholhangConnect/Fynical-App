## üì∏ How to Take Screenshots

### On Android Device

**Method 1: Hardware Buttons**
1. Navigate to the screen you want to capture
2. Press **Power + Volume Down** simultaneously
3. Hold for 1-2 seconds
4. Screenshot saved to Gallery

**Method 2: Quick Settings**
1. Swipe down notification panel
2. Tap **Screenshot** icon
3. Screenshot captured

**Method 3: Google Assistant**
1. Say "Hey Google, take a screenshot"
2. Screenshot captured

### On Android Emulator (Android Studio)

**Method 1: Toolbar Button**
1. Run app in emulator
2. Click camera icon in emulator toolbar
3. Screenshot saved automatically

**Method 2: Keyboard Shortcut**
- **Windows/Linux:** `Ctrl + S`
- **Mac:** `Cmd + S`

**Method 3: Extended Controls**
1. Click **...** (three dots) in emulator toolbar
2. Go to **Camera** tab
3. Click **Take Screenshot**
